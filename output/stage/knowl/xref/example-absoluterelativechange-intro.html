Example 3.1.13. Percentage Change.

The value of a car dropped from $7400 to $6800 over the last year. What percent decrease is this?
🔗
Solution.
Here, the piece we’re looking for is the percent. The part is the amount the quantity has changed, \(\$7400-\$6800=$600\text{,}\) and the whole is the starting amount, $7800.
🔗
\begin{align*} \text{PERCENT} \amp = \frac{\text{PART}}{\text{WHOLE}}\\ \amp = \frac{\$600}{\$7400}\\ \amp = 0.081081\dots = 0.\overline{081}\\ \amp \approx 8.1\% \end{align*}
The price has dropped by 8.1%.
