Fact 2.1.18. Equivalent Fractions.

🔗
Any fraction, proper or improper or as a fractional piece of a mixed number, can be adjusted to an equivlent fraction by multiplying or dividing both the numerator and the denominator by the same non-zero number.
🔗
This tells us the allowable “moves” we can do to a fraction without changing its value.
