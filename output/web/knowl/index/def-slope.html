Definition 2.3.2. Slope Formula.

Given two ordered pairs \((x_1, y_1)\) and \((x_2, y_2)\text{,}\) the slope of the line connecting the points is given by
\begin{gather*} m = \frac{\text{rise}}{\text{run}} = \frac{\text{change in output}}{\text{change in input}} = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} \end{gather*}
